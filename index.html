Avijit Dasgupta

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at IIIT Hyderabad, India and a member of CVIT lab, jointly advised by C. V. Jawahar, IIIT Hyderabad, and Karteek Alahari at THOTH group, Inria. My research focuses on video recognition under limited supervision.

In the past, I've had the good fortune to spend three wonderful months as a visiting researcher at the THOTH group, Inria.

My Ph.D. is funded by the Google India Ph.D. Fellowship, 2017 (1 out of 4 recipients). Thank you Google!
